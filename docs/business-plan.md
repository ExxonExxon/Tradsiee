# Tradsiee: Business Plan

**Founder:** Tomas Gorjux  
**Location:** Melbourne, Australia  
**Commencement:** 2026

---

## 🎯 Mission Statement
At **Tradsiee**, our mission is to eliminate the guesswork for tradespeople. We empower tradies to understand the job site *before* they arrive, significantly reducing wasted travel time and the frustration of missing parts. By enabling customers to upload descriptive videos, we provide tradies with a clear visual of the task at hand—ensuring every process is smoother, faster, and more efficient.

## 🔭 Vision
Within the next five years, Tradsiee will evolve from an innovative tool into the national industry standard. We envision a future where Tradsiee is the primary recommendation for plumbing, electrical, and all trade-based services across Australia. 

Unlike competitors who rely solely on automated bots, we are committed to **human-centric customer support** to build genuine trust. We will pair this personal touch with a world-class, lightning-fast infrastructure that stays ahead of the curve through constant updates.

## 🏆 Strategic Goals
1.  **Scale:** Onboard 100+ active businesses onto the Tradsiee platform.
2.  **Sustainability:** Achieve full profitability by optimizing operational efficiency.
3.  **Advocacy:** Build a product so essential that it becomes the #1 tool tradies recommend to their peers.

## 🚀 Key Business Objectives
Our immediate priority is to secure our first **10 pilot users**. We will work closely with these tradespeople to gather intensive feedback, refining the product until it delivers a seamless, high-value experience that exceeds their expectations.
